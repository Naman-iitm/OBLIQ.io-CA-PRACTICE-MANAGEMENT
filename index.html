07 — Frequently asked questions

Your questions, answered honestly.

Q Will my clients actually use this?
Yes — and more readily than you expect. Clients receive a simple WhatsApp link with their document checklist. It works on mobile, no app download needed. Most clients appreciate having one clear place to submit, rather than sending files to different numbers and emails. The firms we've spoken to report strong client adoption within the first filing cycle.
Q Is it complicated? Will my team need training?
No. The interface is designed to feel familiar — like a simple task list, not enterprise software. Most article assistants pick it up in one sitting. We also provide a short onboarding session for your team at no extra charge. If you can manage a WhatsApp group, you can manage Obliq.io.
Q Will this replace Tally?
No — and it's not trying to. Tally is excellent for bookkeeping and you should continue using it. Obliq.io sits on top of your existing tools: it handles client communication, document collection, task tracking, and deadline management — the coordination layer that Tally doesn't cover. They work alongside each other.
Q How long does it take to set up?
Most firms are running within 2–3 days. Day 1: import your client list. Day 2: set up compliance checklists and team members. Day 3: send your first automated reminders. We handle the technical setup with you. You don't need to be tech-savvy — we do the heavy lifting.
Q Is my client data safe?
Absolutely. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We use bank-level security standards. Client documents are accessible only to your firm and the client themselves — not shared with any third party. We are fully compliant with Indian data protection requirements. You retain full ownership of all your data, always.
Q What if I have 50 clients, not 200?
It works at any size. Smaller firms benefit immediately from document tracking and automated reminders. As you grow, the task assignment and billing features become essential. The pricing is designed to be fair at every scale — you pay for what you use.
